A faster way to check freight profitability.

HaulMargin is designed for the real moment when a broker sends a load, the clock is moving, and you need a clean answer without opening a spreadsheet.

01

Enter the lane and offered pay

Name the load, add the load date, enter pickup and dropoff addresses, and compare the offered pay against your target profit.

02

Estimate route miles

Use Apple Maps mileage estimates for loaded miles and deadhead miles from your origin or current location, then add tolls when needed.

03

See the decision

Review fuel, tolls, fixed costs, maintenance, driver pay, dispatch, factoring, net profit, profit per mile, and minimum acceptable rate.

Built around the way loads are evaluated.

HaulMargin keeps the calculation simple enough for a quick lane check, but detailed enough to catch the costs that quietly eat margin.

CostsDiesel price, MPG, maintenance per mile, fixed cost per mile, dispatch, factoring, and driver pay.
DecisionBook, negotiate, or reject based on net profit and the target profit you set.
MemorySave named load estimates so repeat lanes, broker offers, and past decisions are easy to reopen.

Important: HaulMargin provides estimates for planning and comparison. Route miles, tolls, fuel prices, operating costs, customer payments, accessorials, detention, layover, weather, and actual driving conditions can vary. Confirm details with your dispatcher, broker, shipper, carrier, tax professional, or other qualified advisor before making business decisions.
