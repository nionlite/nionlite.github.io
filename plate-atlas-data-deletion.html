Plate Atlas

Data Deletion

Last updated: May 19, 2026

This page explains how Plate Atlas users can delete app data or request help from NiONlite LLC. Plate Atlas does not require a NiONlite account and NiONlite does not operate a hosted user profile or food journal database for the app.

Quick summary: Most Plate Atlas data is stored on your device, in your private Apple iCloud account when iCloud sync is enabled, or in a backup file you choose to save to Google Drive or another storage provider. You can delete most of this data directly from the app, your device, iCloud, Google Drive, or the storage provider you selected.

Delete Data in the App

You can delete or reset Plate Atlas data from inside the app where those controls are available:

  • Delete saved dishes, safe-for-me entries, restaurant notes, and post-meal notes from the My Dishes or saved dish screens.
  • Clear scan history from the Settings or History area where available.
  • Reset your dietary profile, allergens, disliked ingredients, app language, and related preferences from Settings or Profile where available.
  • Delete exported Android backup files from Google Drive or any other storage provider where you saved them.

Delete Data from Your Device or Cloud Account

  • Uninstalling Plate Atlas removes local app data from many devices, subject to the operating system's backup and restore settings.
  • On Apple devices, you can manage or remove iCloud app data using Apple iCloud settings if iCloud sync is enabled.
  • On Android, you can clear app storage in Android Settings, uninstall the app, and delete any exported backup file from Google Drive or your selected storage provider.

Request Help with Deletion

To request help deleting data associated with Plate Atlas, email us:

Email: support@nionlite.com

Subject: Plate Atlas Data Deletion Request

Please include the platform you use, such as iOS, iPadOS, Android, TestFlight, or Google Play testing. Do not include sensitive medical information in your email.

What We Can Delete or Help With

  • Support emails or support records you sent to NiONlite, where we can reasonably identify them.
  • Any NiONlite-controlled records that can reasonably be associated with your request.
  • Guidance for deleting local app data, private iCloud data, Android app storage, or user-created Google Drive backup files.

What May Be Kept

  • Data stored only on your device, in your private iCloud account, or in your Google Drive or selected storage provider may not be accessible to NiONlite and must be deleted by you through the app, device, or provider controls.
  • AI proxy rate-limit records, abuse prevention logs, diagnostics, billing entitlement records, and security records may be retained for a limited period where needed for fraud prevention, security, app reliability, legal compliance, or dispute handling.
  • Apple, Google, Cloudflare, AdMob, Gemini, Workers AI, OpenAI, Google Drive, and other third-party providers may retain data according to their own policies and account controls.
