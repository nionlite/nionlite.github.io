Compare credit union mortgage rates by state availability.

Credit union mortgage rates are only useful if the borrower can realistically qualify for the lender. CUB helps users narrow published rates with state selection, lender details, membership context, and favorites.

What state filtering helps solve

Mortgage shoppers often find attractive credit union rates that are not realistic because of geography or membership requirements. State filtering helps reduce noise before users spend time contacting lenders.
