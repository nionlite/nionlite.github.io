Built for real life

One reminder is not enough when the deadline is real.

ByWhen is designed for tasks that need judgment, not just a single alarm. It scores open time against practical constraints and keeps nudging when a reminder is dismissed.

01

Deadline risk

The closer the due date gets, the more urgent the plan becomes. ByWhen shows when a task is on track, at risk, or critical.

02

Task effort

A 10 minute call and a 3 hour writing session should not be scheduled the same way. ByWhen uses estimated duration and minimum session length.

03

Task type

Deep work, admin, study, calls, and out-and-away errands get different scheduling behavior because they need different kinds of time.

04

Calendar blocks

Apple Calendar events can become busy windows that ByWhen avoids when choosing when to nudge you next.

05

Sleep and work rhythm

Your wake time, sleep time, work hours, and workdays help ByWhen avoid bad times and protect the day around your real routine.

06

Reminder style

Choose a task-specific nudge style, from gentle to persistent, so the app matches the seriousness of the work.

Adaptive nudges

Dismissed does not mean forgotten.

ByWhen is made for the moment when you tap later. It reconsiders the task, the remaining time, and your schedule before deciding when to come back.

Private by design

Your schedule should feel personal because it stays personal.

ByWhen is privacy focused by design. Your tasks, deadline plans, rhythm settings, calendar blocks, and local backups stay on your device. Data does not go to NiONlite servers for planning, and backup files only leave the device if you choose to export them to your own iCloud Drive or another destination.
